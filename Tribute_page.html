Benson Idahosa

Tribute Of A Great Man of God Benson Idahosa
  • Born 11 September, 1938 Benin City, Edo State
  • In 1971 he earned a diploma in Divinity from Christ for the Nation's Institite in Dallas, Texas
  • Married Archbishop Margaret Idahosa and were blessed with 4children, 1son and 3daughters
  • In 1971 he established churches all over Nigeria and Ghana
  • He was the founding president of Pentecostal Fellowship of Nigeria (PFN)
